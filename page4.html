HOW IT STARTED

Augmented Reality has been Creeping Up on Us

It started with a twist...BMW Started a mass maketing of augmented reality 10 years ago! If you've ever wondered what the future of mechanics is, it is augmented reality. 10 years ago, BMW made a program that helped it's mechanics know where every screw and bolt was on their cars. This lead to the mechanics knowing not only what they had worked on but what they would have to work on in the future. Gone was sending your mechanics two states away to learn how to work on a car and here was the technology to have a classroom in your shop. 

But is it finacially sound? This is the question all mechanics ask me. We are not going to be able to go directly from a hands on experience to a virtual or even augmented one. People need more time to change.
